简介

中国传统建筑屋顶是中国古代建筑最富有艺术魅力的组成部分之一，它不仅起到遮风挡雨的实际作用，还具有很高的美学价值。中国古代常见的屋顶基本有以下几种：庑殿顶、歇山顶、硬山顶、悬山顶、攒尖顶、卷棚顶、盝顶等。这些屋顶样式在北京故宫中多有体现。北京故宫是世界上现存规模最大、建筑精美、保存最完整的大规模建筑群，从各个方面无不体现着中国古代建筑之精华。本网站将以图文形式讲解故宫建筑中常见的三种屋顶样式：庑殿顶、歇山顶与攒尖顶，供大家学习交流。

As one of the most appealing components of ancient Chinese architecture, the roofs of traditional Chinese buildings not only serve as shelter from the elements, but also boast considerable aesthetic value. The common patterns of traditional roofs are basically as follows: hip roof, gable and hip roof, overhanging gable roof, pyramidal roof, round ridge roof and even roof. These patterns can all be found in the Palace Museum in Beijing. The Beijing Palace Museum is the largest, the most delicate and most complete ancient wooden architectural complex in the world, revealing the cream of ancient Chinese palace architecture. With images and paragraphs for your reference, this website will elucidate three roof patterns that can be easily noticed in the Palace Museum: hip roof, gable and hip roof, and pyramidal roof.
